Payment & Refund Policy

This policy applies to products and services provided through DiaryOfAFarmer.co.uk.

Payment Methods

We accept payments via PayPal for all products and services, including digital downloads, consulting services, and physical poultry equipment.

All transactions are processed in USD ($).

When Payment Is Taken

Digital products (ebooks, audiobooks, and consulting services): payment is collected immediately at checkout. Access to your product or booking confirmation is provided after successful payment.

Physical products (affiliate or drop-shipping items): payment is collected immediately to confirm your order and initiate delivery.

Payment Security

All transactions are securely processed through PayPal. We do not store your payment details.

You may be redirected to PayPal's secure platform to complete your purchase.

Order Confirmation

A confirmation email is sent once payment is successfully processed.

For digital products, this includes download or access instructions.

For consulting services, this confirms your booking details.

For physical products, this includes delivery estimates and tracking information (if available).

Unsuccessful Payments and Cancellations

Orders with unsuccessful payments are not processed.

For consulting bookings, unpaid or canceled appointments may be rescheduled at our discretion.

Digital Products and Consulting Refunds

All digital downloads are non-refundable due to instant delivery.

If you experience technical access issues, contact solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk. Support responds within 48 hours.

Consulting services may be canceled with at least 48 hours' notice. Late cancellations may not be eligible for a refund.

Physical Product Returns and Refunds

You may request a refund or replacement if your product is faulty, damaged, or not as described.

Return requests must be made within 14 days of receiving the product by emailing solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk with your order number and issue description.

Refunds are issued via the original payment method after confirmation of product return.

Return shipping costs may apply unless the product is faulty.

General Conditions

Refunds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

We reserve the right to refuse refunds for items that do not meet the conditions above.

Affiliate Product Disclaimer

Some marketplace products are provided via affiliate partnerships.

Delivery, warranty, and returns are subject to the supplier's policies.

We may earn a commission from linked products at no extra cost to you.

Payment Support

For payment-related concerns, contact solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk.
