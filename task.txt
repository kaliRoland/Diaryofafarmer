Payment Policy – DiaryOfAFarmer.co.uk

Payment Methods
We accept payments via PayPal for all products and services, including digital downloads, consulting services, and physical poultry equipment. All transactions are processed in GBP (£).

When Payment is Taken
	•	Digital Products (ebooks, audiobooks, consulting services): Payment is collected immediately at checkout. Access to your product or booking confirmation will be provided after successful payment.
	•	Physical Products (affiliate or drop-shipping items): Payment is also taken immediately to confirm your order and initiate delivery.

Payment Security
All transactions are securely processed through PayPal. We do not store your payment details. You may be redirected to PayPal’s secure platform to complete your purchase.

Order Confirmation
A confirmation email will be sent once your payment is successfully processed:
	•	For digital products, this email includes download or access instructions.
	•	For consulting services, this email confirms your booking details.
	•	For physical products, it includes delivery estimates and tracking information (if available).

Unsuccessful Payments & Cancellations
	•	Orders with unsuccessful payments will not be processed.
	•	For consulting bookings, unpaid or canceled appointments may be rescheduled at our discretion.

Contact for Payment Issues
For any payment-related concerns, contact us at solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk.

Return & Refund Policy – DiaryOfAFarmer.co.uk

Digital Products (ebooks, audiobooks, consulting services)
	•	All digital downloads are non-refundable due to their instant delivery.
	•	If you experience technical issues accessing your purchase, please contact solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk. Our support team will assist within 48 hours.
	•	Consulting services may be canceled with at least 48 hours notice. Late cancellations may not be eligible for a refund.

Physical Products (Affiliate & Drop-Shipped Poultry Equipment)
	•	You may request a refund or replacement if your product is faulty, damaged, or not as described.
	•	Return requests must be made within 14 days of receiving the product by emailing solomon@diaryofafarmer.co.uk with your order number and issue description.
	•	Refunds will be issued via the original payment method after confirmation of the product return.
	•	Shipping costs for returns may apply unless the product is faulty.

General Conditions
	•	Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis.
	•	We reserve the right to refuse refunds for items that do not meet the above conditions.
